Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2025

Aurelia Labs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a live speech and grammar correction app that allows users to speak in their target language and receive AI-assisted corrections. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your data is used.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email, username, and password (securely stored)
  • Speech transcriptions and AI-generated grammar corrections
  • Correction history

We do not collect audio recordings, analytics, or location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide grammar correction services
  • Allow access to your correction history
  • Authenticate your account

We do not use your data to train AI models or for advertising.

3. How Data Is Processed

Speech is transcribed using Whisper, then processed by Ollama for grammar correction. Processing happens on our backend servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Transcription and correction are performed using internal tools (Whisper, Ollama).

5. Data Retention

Transcriptions and corrections are stored indefinitely so users can access their history. Audio is not stored. You may delete your account and data at any time.

6. User Rights

You may delete your account or correction history from within the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is intended for users aged 13 and older. By using the app and creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not create an account. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us at contactaurelialabs@gmail.com, and we will delete the information promptly.

8. International Users

By using our services, you consent to the processing of your information as described here.

9. Contact Us
